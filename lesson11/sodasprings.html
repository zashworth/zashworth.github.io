Soda Springs Gysers

Todd Johnson

The Soda Springs gysers and hot springs are a great place to go experience the wonders of Idaho. They are beautiful and the gysers go off every 45 minutes. The hot springs and gysers are home to some very special species of micro-organisms. But to add the awesomeness of the area, Soda Springs is also home to a swimming pool that is heated by the natural hotsprings of the area. This is super cost effective and a fun experience for anyone looking to go swimming.

Soda Springs is also a great place for any other outdoor adventures from hiking to camping. The local area offers some amaxing trails and some super nice campgrounds. If you are lucky you may even see deer and moose hanging out in the open close to the mountains. Don't forget to stop by Soda Springs on your way to wherever you are going!
