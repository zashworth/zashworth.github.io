Hish Haven Fishing Tournament

John Locke

The annual Fish Haven Fishing Tournament was a super success this year. We had over 150 anglers turnout for this awesome event. The winner was a lady from close by in Paris, Idaho. She caught the biggest fish at 10.4 pounds! It was a mature rainbow trout with awesome coloratin. As the winner she won an all expenses paid fihing trip to Alaska to fish for King Salmon during thier spawning season. The second runner up was a gentleman from Colorado. He won a new rod and tacklebox filled with all the goodies.

They are planning on hosting this same tournament next year as well and hope that it will be even bigger. Later thsi year they will be hosting a local fly fishing tournament for those fly fishermen. They hope to see a great turnout and have some awesome prizes available at that one too. Not to mention free food and some great entertainment!
