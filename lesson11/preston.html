Preston City Picnic

Susan Smith

The Preston City Picnic was a huge success. We had about 60% of the residents show up and bring food to share with all. Everyone was having a blast with the great food and all the games that were supplied by local businesses. We had corn hole, horseshoes, volleyball, soccer, football, and even a dunk tank that was manned by the mayor! If you missed it this year we will be doing another picnicn year around the same time. We will be sending out a reminder through the local businesses and flyers.

To end the wonderful picnic we had an afternoon concert put on by "The Stingers". It was great family fun that was ended with a small but enjoyable fireworks show put on by the city. As an added bonus many businesses had giveaways that you could enter into. The local backery also put on a baking competition that was judged by the local fire department. It was such a blast. We are excited for next year!
