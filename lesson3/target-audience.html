Target Audience Description

The target audience is someone who has a love for outdoor activites and is concious of what the weather will be each day so that they can plan ahead and be prepared for each day and for each activity. They will most likely either have family or friends and will be over the age of 20. They will use phones or tablets to look up the weather more than computers, so the website needs to be built with that in mind.

Two Personas

  • Persona 1

    • The first persona that will visiting the website will most likely be a male or female between the ages of 21-30. This person will either be a single college student or recenlty married within the last 5 years. They will probably have a small family with very young children, all under 10 years old. They will not be making much money, probably only between $15,000 - $30,000 a year. They will either be enrolled in school, recently graduated, or they never went to college. They will also be avid outdoor lovers. They will using the site to see what the weather is to be able to do cheap activites outside because of their love of the outdoors and lack of money. THey will most likely be accessing the site on a mobile device, like a phone or tablet. They will be accessing the app in the mornings and possibly during the day while planning activities.

  • Persona 2

    • The second person that will be visiting the website will be a male or female between the ages of 30-55. This person will most likely have a family and will have a career that they are working in each day. They will be making around $45,000 - $75,000 a year and will be living in a house. They will probably older children and even possibly young grandkids. They love the outdoors adn their family. They will be using the site to check weather for family activities and for traveling for vacations or visiting other family members. They will probably check the site early in the mornings or late at night before bed. This will probably occur on a mobile device. But they may access the site while at work, which in that case will be on a computer.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1

    • A 22 year old college student arrives at the website through his phone. He is looking for the weather forcast for Saturday because he wants to take a date to hike the Menan Butte. He can see on the home page the 5 day weather forcast for the Rexburg, Idaho area. He clicks on it and goes to a seperate page were he can more information than just the basic high/low temps and if it is sunny or not. On the second page he is able to look closer and see if there is a chnce of rain or not. He is satisfied with what he found. And leaves the site happy, willing to return.

  • Scenario 2

    • A 25 year old married woman arrives at the website through her phone because she is planning an outside birthday party for her husband. She searches through the available locations until she finds her area and click on it to see an extended 10 day forcast, since his birthday is in a week. She puruses the forcasted weather and sees that the weather is supposed to be bad. So she bookmarks the site to keep tabs on the weather if it changes. She returns daily to check and see if the weather changes so she can throw an outside Birthady party.

  • Scenario 3

    • A 30 year old mother wakes up early to check the weather. It is December and she knows that it is cold outside so she is checking the temperature to see if it is cold enough for the schools to be canceled so she can know if she has to get her kids up or not. She notices that on the front page it is giving the current temperature and there are recent news articles stating that schools will be canceled in teh area because fo a severe wind chill. She is happy because she got her answers and then goes and makes herself some hot cocoa to warm up.

  • Scenario 4

    • A 40 year old father is checking the weather on his phone just before he goes to bed because he wants to take his son to a football game the next day but he wants to know if it will be cold or not. He searches for his town and finds the extended forcast. He realizes that the weather will cold but decent. So he decides to wake his son up in the morning and tell him the good news. The father bookmarks the site in his phone for future reference.

  • Scenario 5

    • A 53 year old woman is looking up the weather to be able to plan her family's reunion in the next week. She find her city on the home page and goes to the weather page for the extended forcast. There she notices that the weather in supposed to be nice so she goes ahead with her plans for the reunion. But she bookmarks the page to be able check every day just in case the weather changes. She is happy that she will be able to spend the tiem with her family outside.
